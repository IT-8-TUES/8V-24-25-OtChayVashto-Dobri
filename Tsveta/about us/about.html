Who are we

Welcome to Leaf & Bloom, where every cup tells a story. We are passionate about bringing you the finest teas from the Bulgarian mountains - handpicked, ethically sourced and grown with care. Whether you are a seasoned tea lover or just beginning your journey, our mission is to share the warmth, comfort, and health benefits of real tea.

Our tea is made the traditional way - simple, honest, and deeply comforting after a long hike.

  • Cultivated by remote mountain families, our tea plants thrive on crystal-clear, mineral-rich water drawn straight from nearby glacial springs—water that nourishes every leaf long before the first harvest.

  • These herbs, often handpicked from the surrounding mountains or brought from nearby villages, include wild mint, thyme, chamomile, and the prized Sideritis (commonly known as mountain tea).

  • Each herb brings its own unique character: mint offers a refreshing coolness, thyme lends earthy depth, chamomile provides gentle floral notes, and mountain tea contributes a subtly sweet, grassy aroma revered for its calming properties.

  • The tea is served hot, often with a spoonful of honey (sometimes local) or sugar, and sometimes accompanied by a slice of lemon or traditional Bulgarian jam.

At the heart of what we do is a simple goal: to connect you directly with the people who have tended these mountain herbs for generations. By working closely with small-scale growers and foragers in remote regions, we help preserve their traditions while bringing their exceptional teas to a wider audience.

Every cup you enjoy supports local communities, celebrates ancient knowledge, and honors the natural rhythms of the mountain landscape. We're proud to be the link between the hands that harvest and the hearts that savor.
