Whether you're a seasoned tea lover or just beginning your journey,our mission is to share the warmth, comfort, and healthful benefits of real tea.

At the high-altitude mountain shelter of Zaslon Tevno Ezero,tea is made the traditional way - simple, honest, and deeply comforting after a long hike.

  1. Fresh mountain water is collected from nearby glacial sources or springs - cold, clear, and mineral-rich.
  2. Water is boiled over a gas stove or wood-burning stove inside the hut, which adds a cozy, alpine atmosphere to the preparation.
  3. Locally sourced or brought dried herbs such as mint, thyme, mountain tea and other, or chamomile are steeped in the hot water.
  4. The tea is served hot, often with a spoonful of honey (sometimes local) or sugar, and sometimes accompanied by a slice of lemon or traditional Bulgarian jam.
